Who we are

We are enthusiastic people who are passionate about what we do. AlphaNova game development company was set up in 2019 at Dnipro city by a group of wizards from the game development world.

The founders dreamed of the software development practice since their school years. While developing the career our founders and staff got their experience at the leading game development companies and studios, which helped them to gain a deep knowledge of Unity, Unreal Engine, Marmalade, Corona SDK, MS Azure, C#, Node.js and other technologies.

All their experience and passion for computer games development allowed them to assemble the first-class team in 2019 and set up AlphaNova game development company.
